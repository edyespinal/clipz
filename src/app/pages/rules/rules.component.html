Rules

The whole point is to see how many songs you can guess.

Rules are simple:

  1. You get to hear a few seconds of the beginning of a song. *
  2. Guess the name of the song (no need for the artist's name).**
  3. If you get it right, you get a new song and some points.
  4. ...That's it! Easy, no?

* The higher the difficulty, the less seconds you get to hear the song but you get a points multiplier when you get it right!
** If you don't know the song, you can skip it but you lose a heart.
